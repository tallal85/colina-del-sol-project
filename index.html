Graffiti-to-Murals: Building Community Resilience in Colina del Sol

Transforming vandalized walls into vibrant community art spaces

Project Overview

This initiative focuses on converting graffiti-covered walls in Colina del Sol into beautiful murals, fostering community pride, safety, and cultural representation. By working with local artists, residents, and city partners, we aim to create lasting visual landmarks that inspire and engage.
